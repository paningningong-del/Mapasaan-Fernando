What does this website aim to achieve?

Mapasaan Fernando is a student-made website, which aims to highlight local tourist attractions in San Fernando, Pampanga, to enable tourists to have easy access to information, directions, and organise their travel plans.

How do I report an error or incorrect information?

To report any errors, bugs, or inaccurate information on the website, you may contact our official Gmail account, mapasaanfernando@gmail.com

Which browsers/devices are compatible?

As this website is still a prototype, it is only compatible with desktop devices as of the moment.

Is the website's information about the establishments presented accurate, reliable, and verified?

All information regarding the featured establishments is taken directly from their respective official websites and has undergone thorough research and verification by the researchers.
