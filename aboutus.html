About Us

Mapasaan Fernando is a student-made, web-based interactive mapping platform that brings travel details into one system, mainly it presents nearby tourist spots, categorised for easier navigation. Featuring numerous destinations around San Fernando, Pampanga, with up-to-date information to help users make informed decisions. Additionally, it provides a booking feature, displaying prices, recommended destinations, and a regular routing feature with travel time. This software will aim to provide tourists with a smooth and comfortable travel experience, contributing to tourism in Pampanga, specifically in San Fernando.
